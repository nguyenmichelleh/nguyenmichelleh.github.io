Behind Lil' Cakery

Everett, WA





Hello! Thank you so much for visiting.



This business is a product of my passion for creating cakes and cupcakes that capture the invaluable quality of home-baked goods. To me, a cake is the opportunity to bring someone's story, vision, or celebration to life through art and flavor.

The art of baking was instilled in me as a child and has always carried a sense of nostalgia and comfort. I grew up in a home where there was always a cake on the counter. My mother would bake when she was happy, when she was sad, when it was a hot summer day and she had 30 minutes before she needed to leave the house.

I have been baking and decorating customized cakes and cupcakes for 10 years, continually applying modern techniques and growing as a baker with each customer I serve. Each component of my cakes are made from scratch, with every bit of detail and care going into them.

Cake is an exciting experience, from designing it to taking the first bite, which is something I want each of my customers to feel. My goal is to match your vision with a delicious work of art.



I look forward to hearing from you!

Lilli Flood
