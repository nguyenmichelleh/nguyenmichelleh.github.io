Cakes

Each cake is custom made and designed. Submit any photos of other creations that you would like your cake to resemble, browse the gallery, or visit Instagram to select a design you would like to have recreated, or simply describe a look you have in mind! Choose from the flavors list or let me know if there is another flavor combination you desire for your order. Please note that pricing includes labor and all supplies.

Wedding Cakes

Wedding cakes are custom and it is challenging to provide a base price. Simple cakes frosted with buttercream begin at $5.00 per serving. Cakes frosted with buttercream and covered in fondant begin at $7.00 per serving. Wedding cakes drastically vary in price depending on the decorations, design, flavor, and fillings chosen. When inquiring about a wedding cake, please provide as much detail as possible!



Geode Cakes

Geode cakes are very popular! These cakes are decorated with rock candy and painted with coloring and gold luster dust to resemble the inside of an actual geode. To create depth and dimension, a chunk of cake is removed before it is decorated, resulting in fewer servings which you will notice below. Cakes can be made in any color and are constructed like a basic cake with 4 layers of cake and 3 layers of filling.
