Contact

To get in contact with me, please either complete and submit the form below, e-mail me at lilcakery@gmail.com, or call/text my direct phone number at 425-478-0121. I will respond within 1-2 business days. When contacting please provide as much detail as possible including the date that you need the order, the type of event the order is for, quantity of cupcakes or size of cake needed, flavor(s) desired, inspirational photos that you would like me to keep in mind when designing your order, and ANY other details. Thank you, I look forward to hearing from you!


Cake Tasting

Cake tastings are available upon request. The price of this service varies depending on how many flavors are chosen.
